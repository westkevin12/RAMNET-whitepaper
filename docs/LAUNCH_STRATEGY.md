# RAMNET Launch Strategy: Utility Pre-Funding & Bootstrap Liquidity

This document outlines the strategy to launch the **$RAM** token with intrinsic value and initial liquidity, focusing on **Pre-Paid Utility** rather than speculative capital.

## 1. Utility Pre-Funding Phase

To ensure the token has value from Day 1, we implement a Utility Pre-Funding phase. This allows the community to fund the mesh's initial "fuel" (liquidity).

### The Crowdfund Mechanism

- **Contribution:** Users contribute PLS or stablecoins to a Genesis Smart Contract.
- **Allocation:** Contributors receive $RAM tokens at a designated **Genesis Rate**.
- **Liquidity Lock:** 100% of collected funds are mathematically locked into the initial **$RAM/WPLS** liquidity pool on a PulseChain DEX.

### Pre-Paid Compute Credits

This phase is marketed as a **Pre-Purchase of Compute**. Funders are not buying equity; they are buying the right to use the mesh at a significant discount compared to future market rates.

## 2. Tiered Genesis Rewards

Rewards are structured to ensure that the initial token holders are also the primary **Compute Providers**.

- **Hardware Commit Bonus:** Higher $RAM allocations are granted to users who register verifiable hardware specs (NVIDIA Rubin, Mythic Analog pods) during pre-funding.
- **Locked Utility Period:** Tokens from this phase are vested for 90 days, during which they can **only be used to pay for compute tasks** on the mesh. This prevents immediate sell pressure and ensures the mesh is used for its intended purpose.

## 3. Floor Price via the "Joule Standard"

To provide a non-speculative valuation, $RAM is anchored to the physical cost of compute:

- **SCU Anchoring:** One $RAM is pegged to the average energy/operational cost of delivering one **Standard Compute Unit (SCU)**.
- **Digital Commodity:** This positioning treats $RAM as a "Digital Fuel" (like gas or electricity), providing a logical floor price based on the thermodynamic reality of matrix operations on Rubin/Mythic hardware.

## 4. Tactical Roadmap

1. **Genesis Contract Deployment:** Deploy the simple contribution contract on PulseChain.
2. **Hardware Dashboard:** Launch `ramnet.io` with a real-time view of "Total Committed Mesh Power."
3. **Whitepaper Release:** Synchronize the public reveal with the Genesis Contract launch to capture and direct immediate interest into liquidity provisioning.
